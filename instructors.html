About

The Wright instructor for you.

Pass Wright isn't a faceless franchise — it's one dedicated instructor who genuinely cares about getting you on the road safely.

David Wright

Founder & Instructor — Pass Wright Driving Academy

I started Pass Wright because I wanted to do driving lessons differently. Too many learners were telling me about bad experiences — instructors who shouted, rushed them, or just didn't explain things properly.

My approach is simple: teach clearly, stay patient, and make sure every learner walks away from every lesson knowing more than when they got in the car. I specialise in manual tuition and I know the Ipswich, Stowmarket and Bury St Edmunds test routes inside out.

Whether you're 17 and just starting out, or you've had lessons before and need a fresh start — I'll meet you where you are and get you where you need to be.

FAQ

Common questions

Where do you cover?

Across Suffolk — Ipswich, Stowmarket, Bury St Edmunds, Needham Market, and surrounding areas.

Do you teach automatic?

No — Pass Wright is manual only. David specialises in manual tuition to give you the fullest licence and best skill set.

How quickly can I start?

Most learners start within a week. Evenings and weekend slots available.

I'm really nervous — is that ok?

Absolutely. A huge number of David's learners start nervous. Calm, patient teaching is the whole point of Pass Wright.

What car will I learn in?

A modern, dual-control manual car — clean, comfortable, and fitted with a dash cam for optional video reviews.

Can I do an intensive course?

Yes — get in touch and David will build a condensed schedule around your availability.

Ready to learn with David?

Get in touch and let's get your lessons started. First-timers and fresh-starters both welcome.
