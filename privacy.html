Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — kept simple and transparent.

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

Pass Wright Driving Academy is a sole trader driving school operated by David Wright, based in Suffolk, England. Our website address is passwrightdrivingacademy.co.uk.

What data we collect

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (if provided)
  • Your message

We do not collect any data automatically through cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts unless explicitly stated.

How we use your data

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Arrange driving lessons
  • Communicate with you about your lessons

We will never sell, share, or pass your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

How we store your data

Contact form submissions are forwarded directly to our inbox. We do not store your data in any external database. Emails are retained only as long as necessary to fulfil your enquiry and any ongoing lesson arrangement.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

To exercise any of these rights, please use our contact form.

Third-party services

Our contact form is processed via FormSubmit, which forwards your submission to our email without storing your data. You can read their privacy policy at formsubmit.co.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please get in touch via our contact form.
