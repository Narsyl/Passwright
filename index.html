About Pass Wright

Meet David Wright

I'm David, the instructor behind Pass Wright. I set up this school because I believe everyone deserves driving lessons that actually make sense — no jargon, no pressure, just clear teaching that sticks.

Based in Suffolk, I cover Ipswich, Stowmarket, Bury St Edmunds and everywhere in between. I specialise in manual tuition and take real pride in getting my learners test-ready with genuine skill, not just exam tricks. Every lesson is built around you — your pace, your confidence, your goals.

How It Works

Your road to passing

01
Stage One

Foundation

Cockpit drill, clutch control, moving off and stopping. We nail the basics until they're second nature.

02
Stage Two

Road Skills

Junctions, roundabouts, dual carriageways. Real-world driving at a pace that works for you.

03
Stage Three

Test Ready

Mock tests on real routes, manoeuvres polished, and the confidence to walk in knowing you've got this.

04
Stage Four

Pass!

Test day in our dual-control car with everything you've practised. You'll walk in confident, drive well, and come out with your licence.
